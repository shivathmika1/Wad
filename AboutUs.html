About Us

VSports is the first of its kind of multi-sports Academy in east side of Hyderabad. We have all modern and International standard facilities in Outdoor and Indoor sports , majorly being Swimming, Tennis, Badminton, Cricket, Skating, Table Tennis, Box Cricket and Football and Outdoor fitness training. We provide good quality coaching with well experienced and certified coaches. We provide membership facilities and pay and play option. This academy is located in Bandlaguda, Nagole which is convinient location for residents of Nagole, Dilshukhnagar, L.B.Nagar, Mansoorabad, Uppal, Ramanthapur and Vanasthalipuram.
